A quick synopsis...

From a young age I was interested in computing, from hardware to software, even gaming. I spent hours on end building computers, creating small programs, customizing operating systems, and optimizing components. Throughout high school I developed a respect for making things my own, and building computers with optimized systems was where it started...

Initially in college, unsure of a path to take, I chose to get a position in a pharmacy. With interest in Chemistry, I chose to get a degree in Biochemistry - with aspirations of Pharmacy School. Events in my life had changed my original plan, and my real goal was to get a career in something that I was passionate about.

After completing my Bachelor's degree from Winona State University in Biochemistry, I decided to go back to school. I am currently pursuing a 2nd Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from UW - Milwaukee. I plan to find a position in Software and Web Development, as well as back-end programming.
