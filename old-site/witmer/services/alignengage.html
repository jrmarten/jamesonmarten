alignment, engagement and collaboration

    the challenge

    How many employees in your organization "get" the strategy and are truly engaged in their work? In most companies, it is less than 50%. Badly disengaged employees can exceed 20%. You can smell the cash bleeding. To make things worse, silos are too common. Cross-functional collaboration is often compromised by people who are too-busy and avoid controversy.

    Imagine the impact if 90% of your managers and hourly associates were aligned to your strategy, engaged in their work and collaborative!

    benchmarks

    Aligned and engaged employees perform 20% better, on average. Best Buy® measured a $100k increase in operating income per store with each 0.1% increase in engagement!

    On average, 36% of managers lack competence as leaders, harming employee alignment and engagement. Not surprisingly, up to 15% of employees are actively looking to leave their organizations, with a turnover cost of $10-50k each. Sysco® recently realized $50m in cost savings by raising their retention from 65% to 85%.
