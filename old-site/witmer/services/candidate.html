candidate assessment

    If you measure your hiring success, you can improve it. The ROI is huge if you can increase your success rate to 90% or higher. The cost of a mis-hire is typically 5-figures for a front-line supervisor, 6-figures for a Director/VP or salesperson, and 7-figures for a C-suite leader.

    We recommend only 63% of the candidates we assess. Only 36% receive an enthusiastic recommendation. And 20% are potential disasters. Many candidates look great on paper, but are either under-powered, mismatched to the position or have significant rough edges that require substantial development. Even savvy equity firms and boards succeed only half the time they select key executives.

    We can help you succeed over 90% of the time in your hire and promotion decisions, using state-of-the-art simulation, testing and interview methods. Our unique Inbox simulations are highly predictive of strategic, execution and leadership skills. Our psychologists are seasoned, each with years of experience in vetting top talent.

    We tailor assessments for four levels of external candidates or internal staff: CEO/President, Manager/Director/VP, Salesperson and frontline Supervisor.
